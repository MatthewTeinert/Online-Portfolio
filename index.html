About Me

Hey there, I'm Matthew! I'm currently a junior at the University of Texas at Austin studying Finance with a minor in the Analytics and Business of Sports. I'm originally from Round Rock, Texas, which gave me a great foundation growing up in the heart of Texas before making my way to Austin for college.

I'm expected to graduate in May 2027 with my Bachelor of Business Administration from the McCombs School of Business. Currently, I maintain a 3.98 GPA and am part of the McCombs Success Scholars program.

I'm passionate about the intersection of finance and sports, which is why I chose my unique minor. My goal is to break into investment banking or corporate finance, where I can combine my analytical skills with my love for problem-solving.
